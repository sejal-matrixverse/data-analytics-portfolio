# Business Requirements

## Objective

Analyze retail order data to identify sales trends, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance.

## Business Questions

1. What are the total sales and total profit?
2. Which regions generate the highest revenue?
3. Which categories are most profitable?
4. Which products generate the highest sales?
5. How do discounts impact profitability?
6. Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
7. What are the monthly sales trends?
8. Which shipping mode is used the most?

## Deliverables

- SQL Analysis
- Power BI Dashboard
- Executive Summary
- Business Recommendations
